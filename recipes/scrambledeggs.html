Eggs Fruit & Toast

Scroll down

Desription

Eggs Fruit and Toast is a great quick and nutritional breakfast to start your day usually paired with a cup of coffee

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Fruit
  • Toast
  • Butter
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Crack however many eggs you would like into a bowl
  2. Add a pinch of salt and stir
  3. Cover a pan with butter and pour on pan
  4. Stick the toast in the toaster
  5. Scramble your eggs as their cooking
  6. Take the toast out of the toaster and put it on the plate
  7. Pour the scrambled eggs on the plate
  8. Wash your choice of fruit in the sink
  9. Place fruit on plate and company the meal with whatever condamints you would like
