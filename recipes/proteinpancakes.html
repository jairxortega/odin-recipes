Protein Pancakes

Desription

Protein Packed Kodiak pancakes are a great way to get good quality protein in your day

Ingredients

  • Kodiak Pancake Mix
  • One Egg
  • Almond Milk
  • Butter
  • Toppings of your choice

Steps

  1. Pour 1 cup of kodiak pancake mix into a bowl
  2. Crack one egg and add it into the bowl
  3. Pour 3/4 cup of almond milk into bowl
  4. Turn on the stove and cover the pan with some butter
  5. Pour the pancake mix on the pan and cook until your liking
  6. Top the pancakes with whatever floats your boat
