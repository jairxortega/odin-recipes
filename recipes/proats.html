Protein Oats

Desription

Proats stand for Proetin Oats and like the name they are oats that are made with a scoop of the protein of your choice with whatever toppings you would like.

Ingredients

  • Old Fashioned Rolled Oats
  • Protein Powder
  • Brown Sugar
  • Almond Milk
  • Toppings of your choice

Steps

  1. Pour 1 cup of oats into a bowl
  2. Put one scoop of the protein of your choice into the bowl
  3. Put 1/4 tablespoon of brown sugar into bowl
  4. Pour 1 1/4-1/2 cup of almond milk into bowl
  5. stir and put the bowl into the microwave for 1 minute and 30 seconds
  6. Add Whatever toppings you would like and enjoy a warm cup of Proats
